Fitness Software FAQs

Is the website fully responsive?

At this point, only a few of the pages are accessible through mobile phones and Ipads.

Can I create a personal trainer account?

If you are just a client, you cannot have a personal trainer account. On this platform, there are three personal trainers - for beginners, mid level and advanced users. The admin is responsible for creating a trainer account.

Can I book private meetings with my personal trainer?

Yes, you can! If you have any additional question, related to your training program, diet, tasks or other fitness-related queries, you can always book a private meeting through the booking system on the home page of your dashboard. After that your trainer can create a private chatting room where you can ask him wherever you want.

Will I receive dietary guidelines, provided by my personal trainer?

Yes! When you create your account, you need to fill the form which will help us to determine your current level and you will be assigned to the most appropriate personal trainer. Depending on your goals, your coach will provide you with different weekly nutrition plans.
